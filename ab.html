Sofia Grytsenko

I am a User Experience Designer who was born and raised in New Jersey before moving to Rochester, New York for my undergraduate degree. I graduated summa cum laude with a Human-Centered Computing Major with Web Development and Psychology Minor from the Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT). After graduation, I interned at QuidelOrtho as a User Experience Design Intern and found a new appreciation for medical and healthcare systems. This led to my position as a Quality Assurance Analyst at Continual Care Solutions, an application that aids nonprofits in all of their processes. After being involved with the Lucky Flea this year, I am excited to pivot towards fashion and music, and to provide creative solutions to complex problems.
