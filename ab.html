Sofia Grytsenko

Sofia Grytsenko is a User Experience Designer who was born and raised in New Jersey before moving to Rochester, New York for an undergraduate degree. They graduated summa cum laude with a Human-Centered Computing Major with Web Development and Psychology Minor from the Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT). After graduation, Sofia interned at QuidelOrtho as a User Experience Design Intern and found a new appreciation for medical and healthcare systems. This led to their position as a Quality Assurance Analyst at Continual Care Solutions, an application that aids nonprofits in all of their processes. After being involved with the Lucky Flea this year, Sofia is excited to pivot towards fashion and music, and to provide creative solutions to complex problems.
