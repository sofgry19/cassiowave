CyberFit: AR Try-On Mirror

AR Mirror App for Confident Clothing Choices

Project Context

Role:Product Designer & Producer

Timeline: Fall 2024

Tools: Figma

Team: Sofia Grytsenko

CyberFit is an augmented reality (AR) mirror app that empowers users to feel more confident about their clothing choices before making a purchase. By enabling virtual try-ons and personalized style recommendations, CyberFit addresses the gaps left by traditional online shopping platforms.

The Challenge

Online shoppers struggle to visualize how clothes will fit and look on their bodies, relying on static images or model shots that often don’t reflect real fit. This uncertainty causes: decision fatigue, high e-commerce return rates, unsatisfying shopping experiences

The Process

Ideation Prompt:
What if the online shopping experience could be seamlessly combined with your everyday “getting ready” process?

Research & Insights

I started by researching everyday users of online shopping apps to understand their pain points and habits, using interviews and persona development. Competitive analysis revealed that most platforms offered only basic sizing guides or limited “virtual try-on” features, which failed to represent diverse body types.

Exploration & Ideation

I used sketches and user journeys to reimagine a more interactive, supportive shopping experience. These insights guided the development of features that would genuinely improve user confidence and engagement.

Design Development

The Solution

CyberFit empowers users to experiment with clothing through AR mirrors and style inspiration boards, combining personalized recommendations with fully immersive outfit visualization. The prototype, built in Figma, showcased a playful and intuitive interface that helps users visualize and style outfits before making a purchase.

Outcome & Impact

Early user tests showed strong engagement and satisfaction with the way CyberFit eliminated much of the guesswork. Users felt if this procress was present in online stores, they would feel more confident in placing orders.
